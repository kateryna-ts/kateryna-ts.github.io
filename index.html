Brain drain refers to the emigration of skilled workers, often from less developed to more developed countries. This research explores the case of Belarus, where governmental influence of Russia drives economic decisions due to dependency and lack of freedom, ultimately discouraging development.

December, 2025

Brazil's Petrobras plays a pivotal role in global energy politics, balancing between U.S. and Chinese interests. AI & Emerging Technology Research

Blockchain Applications in International Relations

Developing innovative applications of blockchain technology for conflict resolution and international peacekeeping. My research on the 'Universal Cosmopolitan State Blockchain' proposes using immutable ledgers to create transparent historical records that could aid the United Nations in analyzing international conflicts and preventing historical manipulation.

Key Innovation:

Smart contracts based on deontological frameworks to incentivize international cooperation through automated sanctions and diplomatic mechanisms.

AI in Economic Development Analysis

Exploring the potential of artificial intelligence to analyze complex economic migration patterns and predict brain drain effects. Current research focuses on machine learning applications for understanding economic development in post-Soviet states.

Focus Areas:

  • Natural language processing for policy analysis
  • Predictive modeling for migration patterns
  • Automated economic indicator tracking

Machine Learning in Political Science

Investigating how ML algorithms can identify patterns in political behavior, constitutional interpretation, and governance systems. Research includes analysis of Supreme Court decision patterns and prediction of political outcomes.

Applications:

  • Text analysis of legal documents
  • Sentiment analysis of political discourse
  • Pattern recognition in voting behaviors
